Attendee Profile

DataConf targets professionals who work in any area of the data ecosystem, across various different industries. The range of topics aims to attract various levels of experience and roles. The likely attendee is already a data professional wanting to network and learn from other experts in the field, but the event will be designed to also attract people in related fields eager to understand more about data development and potentially identify their next role.

The event targets 150 attendees but can be increased up to the venue’s maximum capacity of 250.
